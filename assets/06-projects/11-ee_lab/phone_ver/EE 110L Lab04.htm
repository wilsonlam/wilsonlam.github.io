Objective

In electrical engineering the oscilloscope is one of the most important device electrical engineers laboratory cannot go without. In this lab we will explore the technical usage for the oscilloscope and measure parameters for the low and high pass alternating current (AC) circuits. This lab consist of two part and will take two weeks to perform. The first objective is to learn the basic function of the oscilloscope and use its ability for measuring voltage, phase shift, time delay, and plotting Lissajous figures for collecting circuits data.

Part B will force the students to operate the oscilloscope and measure the frequency response for simple filter circuits. Student will work with both high-pass and low-pass filter and finding the -3dB location on the dB vs. frequency plot.
